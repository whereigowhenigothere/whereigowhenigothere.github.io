Also, I’m sure the non-miked(?) portions worked great at the original Broadway venue, but unfortunately not so much in a giant touring house like this (and I set in front orchestra). Act 2 went without an issue, PLUS we got to self-upgrade from side to center orchestra, PLUS the remaining audience was much better. The u/s Jud was amazing, especially with him being most affected by the technical issues; it was also fun to see Girl!Mike. The Sis-Hennessy-Benj trio slayed too, along with Sasha Hutchings.
